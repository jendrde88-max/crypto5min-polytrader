# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-02-15

Crypto5min PolyTrader runs locally on your machine/VPS.

## Data the software stores locally

The software stores operational logs on your machine, such as:

- trade logs and status (`logs/`)
- runtime operator overrides (`logs/runtime_config.json`)
- optional dashboard session data

## What the seller receives

By default, the seller does **not** receive your trading data, wallet keys, or logs.

If you contact support, you may choose to share logs or screenshots. Do not share private keys.

## Third-party services

If you enable optional features, the software may call third-party services (e.g., Coinbase public endpoints, Polymarket APIs). Those services have their own privacy policies.
